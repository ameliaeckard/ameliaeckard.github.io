About

My photographic journey began when I received a Polaroid camera after graduating high school. During my time at LSU, I spent over a year capturing scenes I liked — particularly exploring how low light shaped each shot.

What started as a casual interest grew serious after I got my first film camera for Christmas. Since then, I've been steadily developing my craft, experimenting with different film stocks and techniques. Two years in, I’m still exploring and refining the style that feels most like me.
