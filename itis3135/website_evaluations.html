Website Evaluations

Awwwards

Awwwards is a platform that recognizes and showcases innovative and beautifully designed websites. It's a go-to for web design inspiration.

  • Contrast: Awwwards uses sleek, high-contrast typography and image elements to make content pop against subtle backgrounds. There's excellent use of white space and dark/light theming.
  • Repetition: Design elements like headers, image cards, and navigation are consistent across the entire site. Fonts and color schemes are reused to create brand identity.
  • Alignment: Everything is pixel-perfect. Content is grid-aligned with smooth vertical/horizontal spacing, creating visual harmony and clarity.
  • Proximity: Related elements (titles, descriptions, buttons) are grouped logically. It’s very easy to browse and understand each content block at a glance.

Ling's Cars

Ling’s Cars is an infamous website that leases vehicles, but is more well-known for its chaotic and overwhelming design.

  • Contrast: Over-saturated colors, animated backgrounds, and flashing gifs make it painful to look at. There's zero focus — everything is fighting for attention.
  • Repetition: Fonts, icons, and styles are wildly inconsistent. There's no visual pattern, making it hard to tell what's important or where to focus.
  • Alignment: Everything feels randomly thrown together. Text blocks and images are not aligned, creating a messy and confusing layout.
  • Proximity: Different types of content are all crammed together with no clear organization. It’s hard to distinguish offers, contact info, or even the main purpose of the page.
